About me

Hello! ¡Hola! Hej! Ciao!

Welcome, and thanks for visiting my website. My name is Oliver, and I am a molecular biologist (Ph.D.) and data analyst with nearly 20 years of experience in biotech and biopharma research. Over the years, I have acquired solid expertise in data analysis and experimental work, and a passion for using data to uncover insights and make better decisions.

My work has always required critical thinking, project planning, experiment design, methodology documentation, complex dataset analysis, and communicating findings to diverse audiences, skills that I believe are transferrable to different sectors and problems.

I am naturally curious, and I enjoy learning new things, solving problems and exploring new challenges, especially when they involve using data to understand complex systems or address real-world issues.
