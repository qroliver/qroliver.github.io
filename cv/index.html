Professional profile

Molecular biologist (Ph.D.) with a flair for data science and 18+ years of experience in the biotech and biopharma industries. During this time, I have acquired expertise in a broad set of molecular biology skills, which include, among others, high-throughput screenings and genetic engineering, and advanced data analytics of complex biological data (including the analysis of relatively large datasets).

With an analytical and data-driven mindset, I am passionate about data science, and I have a great interest in its potential to transform the world. Throughout my career in scientific research, I have always aimed to bridge the gap between wet-lab research and data analytics, implementing solutions to extract and communicate relevant insights.

Currently on a career break to further develop my data analytics skills and to enjoy time with my family, I am now seeking opportunities that align with my interest in applying my skills to develop data-based solutions to different real-world scenarios. Team player with a natural curiosity, I enjoy learning, taking on new challenges, and continuously growing both professionally and personally.
