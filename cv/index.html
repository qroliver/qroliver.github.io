Professional profile

Molecular biologist (PhD) with a flair for data science and 18+ years of experience in the biotech and biopharma industries. Throughout my career, I have developed a broad portfolio of molecular biology techniques, with deep expertise in high-throughput screenings, genetic engineering, and advanced data analytics of complex biological datasets (including relatively large datasets). These skills and my past experience have allowed me to thrive both in wet-lab and computational settings, and has given me the opportunity to work across multidisciplinary teams, which is something I really enjoy.

I am very interested in advanced data analytics and its potential to transform the world. Throughout my career, with experience in wet-lab molecular biology, exploratory research, assay development, and data analytics, I have always aimed to bridge the gap between hands-on research and computational insights.

Currently on a career break to further develop my data analysis skills and spend time with my family, I'm now looking for opportunities that bring together two of my passions: scientific research and data science. With a curious mindset, I enjoy learning and taking on new challenges, and continuous growth both personally and professionally.
